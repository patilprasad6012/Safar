How to Book Your Tour

Follow these simple steps to plan your perfect trip with Safar

1
Create Your Account

Register with your name, email, and phone number. Your Traveler ID will be generated automatically.

2
Browse & Search Packages

Use the search bar and sort filters on your dashboard to find the perfect destination. Check details, duration, and pricing.

3
Click "Book Now" on a Package

Open the package details page, review the itinerary, check available slots, and click the blue "Book Now" button.

4
Choose a Guide (Optional)

The system will suggest verified local guides. If you want one, click "Yes, Add Guide" — otherwise click "No Thanks".

5
Complete Payment

Choose Credit/Debit Card, UPI, or Net Banking. Review the total amount (including guide fee if selected) and pay securely.

6
Receive Confirmation

You'll receive an SMS and email confirmation with your booking ID. You can also print your digital boarding pass from the dashboard!

Do's
Register with a valid phone number for SMS alerts
Book early — slots are limited
Review package details before paying
Save or print your digital ticket
Consider hiring a local guide for better experience
Don'ts
Don't book the same package twice
Don't share your login credentials
Don't ignore the booking deadline countdown
Don't cancel without reviewing the refund policy
Don't leave special requests empty if you need accommodations

Security Features

How we protect your data and ensure safe travel

Encrypted Passwords

All user passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt encryption. Even our admins cannot read your passwords.

OTP Email Verification

Every new account is verified using a one-time password sent to your email via secure SMTP to prevent fake registrations.

CSRF Protection

All forms are protected with Cross-Site Request Forgery tokens, preventing unauthorized submissions from external sources.

Parameterized Queries

We use parameterized SQL queries to completely eliminate SQL injection attacks on your travel account data.

Role-Based Access Control

Separate login systems for Travelers, Admins, and Guides. Each role can only access features meant for them.

Secure Session Management

Server-side sessions ensure your login state cannot be tampered with. Auto-logout protects you on shared devices.
